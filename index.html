About me

I am a postdoc in Department of Mathematics at Vanderbilt University, supervised by Professor Mark Ellingham. I received my PhD in Mathematics from Georgia Institute of technology under the supervision of Professor Xingxing Yu. I did my undergraduate at USTC mentored by Professor Xinmin Hou. My research interests are structural graph theory, extremal combinatorics, graph coloring and applications in data science and theoretical computer science.
