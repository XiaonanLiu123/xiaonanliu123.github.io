About me

I am currently a postdoctoral scholar in the Department of Mathematics at Vanderbilt University, supervised by Professor Mark Ellingham. I received my PhD in Mathematics from Georgia Institute of technology under the supervision of Professor Xingxing Yu. I did my undergraduate study at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) mentored by Professor Xinmin Hou.

My research interests are structural graph theory, extremal combinatorics, graph coloring and applications in data science and theoretical computer science.
