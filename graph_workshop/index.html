Shanks Workshop on Graph Theory and combinatorics


This is a 2-day conference on recent advances in graph theory & combinatorics. The mini-conference will be held virtually via Zoom on March 12-13th, 2022. No registration is required.

The Zoom Meeting ID is 998 4449 0208. The Meeting Passcord is the first 6 digits of Pi after the decimal.
